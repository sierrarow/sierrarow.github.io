Who I am

I'm a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at UCSD, advised by Deian Stefan. My research sits at the intersection of Security and Programming Languages. I believe in security by construction and making developers' lives easier.

In 2022, I recieved a B.S. in Computer Science at Brown University.

Following graduation, I spent 2.5 years as a Security Engineer at the Naval Undersea Warfare Center, in Newport, RI, securing the computer systems on US Submarines.

Outside of research, I spend time outside looking at all the beautiful things in the world! San Diego is the perfect place to gaze at the ocean and flowers!
